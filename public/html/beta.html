  • Find your happy place!

    Our mission is simple: increase global happiness. In the U.S. alone 16 million people suffer from depression and less than 30 percent are seeking adequate help. At the same time there's mounting evidence that suggests your happiness can be easily elevated by the network effect of those around you. With Happstr you can find your happy place.

    We make a simple web app for you to keep track of your happiness.

    Happster, is a “psychographic location check-in service,” a way to log the emotions you feel when you check in at different places.

    With Happstr you can see when people are delighted, mapped within your area or around the world. Feeling pretty good? Let others know. When you tap on the happy button your elation gets tagged anonymously to a global map. Feeling down? Discover the happiness aroung you and around the world.

    Happstr was built in March 2012 on TheStartupBus from New York to Austin.
